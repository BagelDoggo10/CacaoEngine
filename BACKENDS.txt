List of Cacao Engine backends:

cacaoglglfw:
	What: OpenGL (3.3 Core Profile) + GLFW backend.
	Support: Windows, Mac, and Linux
	Additional Dependencies: Refer to https://www.glfw.org/docs/latest/compile_guide.html#compile_deps for the most up-to-date information
	on GLFW dependencies. When targeting Linux, Cacao Engine will build for both X11 and Wayland, so install both sets of dependencies.
